    About Us

    Let us introduce ourselves

    We are Rachel and Ben Yarrow, a married couple based in the South East of England.

    We met in the summer 2019 in Vassiliki, Greece where our shared love of the ocean and watersports brought us together.

    As two adventure seekers, we have made it our mission to experience every windsurf destination the world has to offer.

    We strongly believe in living conscientiously. Looking after the oceans, looking after our wellbeing and improving mental health through participating in watersports is the way we live our lives.

    Away from the water, we love exploring the UK in our van as well as snowboarding and city breaks in Europe.
